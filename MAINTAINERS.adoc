== How to Become a Maintainer

Maintainers are individuals who have demonstrated sustained high-quality contributions to the project, a deep understanding of its goals and technical aspects, and a commitment to fostering a positive community.

If you are interested in becoming a maintainer, please:

1.  Consistently contribute to the project (code, documentation, reviews).
2.  Demonstrate adherence to the project's Code of Conduct and standards.
3.  Actively participate in discussions and provide constructive feedback.
4.  Express your interest to the existing core maintainers.
